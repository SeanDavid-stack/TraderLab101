# Changelog

## v1.0 — March 25, 2026

### Initial Public Release

**Session Workflow**
- Preflight Checklist with 8 pre-session questions and journal integration
- Pre-Market Levels with BMBridge, CSV file, and Google Sheets import
- Open Context Alignment with statistical decision trees for all 5 open types
- Bias tracking with timestamped change log and reasons
- Live price feed (BMBridge real-time → Yahoo Finance fallback)
- Level tracker with hit logging

**Trade Log**
- 3 scale-outs with R-multiple calculation
- 8 built-in setups (Opening, IBC, IBF, Mean Reversion, VPOC Shift, BAR, MID>VWAP>VPOC, VHVN>VPOC) + custom setups
- Target tracker with 17 chips + custom, price, outcome, bail reasons
- Exit time and auto-calculated duration
- Process rating, execution errors, emotional state tagging
- 11 toggleable, draggable stat cards with daily goals tracker
- Per-instrument commission tracking

**Missed Trades**
- Simple and multi-scale modes
- What If? panel comparing actual vs hypothetical P&L
- Reason breakdown with win/loss split

**Journal**
- Full session entry with auto-filled data
- Bias timeline, preflight snapshot, review mode
- Screenshots, notes, lessons

**Analytics Dashboard**
- 13 overview cards (toggleable, draggable, reorderable)
- 15 chart sections (all toggleable)
- Date range filter (preset + custom)
- Instrument filter
- Commission-adjusted P&L with dual equity curve

**Methodology Reference**
- 9 accordion sections from Tom B.'s methodology
- Statistical hit rates by open type

**Settings**
- Instrument presets with per-instrument commission
- CSV column mapping editor
- Journal dropdown customization
- Daily goals (max loss, max trades, min process rating)
- Full export/import and granular data clearing
