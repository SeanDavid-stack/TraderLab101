⚠️ CRITICAL: Data Safety & Backup Warning

TraderLab 101 is a browser-based application. To ensure your trading history remains secure, you must understand how your data is stored and how to prevent accidental loss.
🖥️ Where is my data stored?

    Your trade logs, screenshots, and custom levels are stored locally in your browser's cache (LocalStorage).

    This data is NOT synced to a cloud or a central database.

    If you "Clear Browser History," "Delete Cookies," or use "Incognito/Private Mode," your entire trading journal will be permanently deleted.

🛡️ How to Protect Your Journal

To keep your work safe, you must manually export your settings and data regularly:

    Export Often: Go to Settings and click Export Data (JSON).

    Save the File: Store this file in a safe place, such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or a local backup folder.

    Update Safely: Before opening a new version (like v2.1), always perform a fresh export of your current data first.

🔄 Recovery

    If you move to a new computer or accidentally clear your browser, you can restore everything by clicking Import Data in the Settings menu.

    Select your most recent backup file to restore your history.

    Remember: If you don't have a backup file and your browser is cleared, your data is gone forever. Export your work at the end of every trading session.
